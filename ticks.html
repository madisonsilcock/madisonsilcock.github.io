Ticks

Ticks are disease vectoring parasites and feed solely on blood from a host. Often these parasites are introduced to humans by their pets.

Tick control is easy, but it requires yard or lawn treatments. Control results are best accomplished by removing all weeds and unwanted brush surrounding your home or property, aslo by treating pets regularly for fleas and ticks, and by having your yard or property treated regularly..
